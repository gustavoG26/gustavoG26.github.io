About Me

My name is Gustavo Guijarro, a Computer Science senior set to graduate in May 2024. My passions lie in data science, cybersecurity, and software engineering, and I've honed my skills in these areas through rigorous coursework and hands-on projects. In my projects, I've developed a car dealership simulation using various object-oriented design patterns and created a safety application for nighttime walks. These experiences have not only improved my technical skills but also my ability to work effectively in a team.

Currently, I am working as an Information Technology Technician at CU OIT Buff Techs, where I provide top-tier technical assistance to CU Boulder students, staff, and faculty. This role has allowed me to use my problem-solving skills to troubleshoot and resolve software and hardware issues.

In addition to my academic and professional pursuits, I'm an active member of the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE), where I participate in bi-weekly meetings with fellow Hispanic engineering students. This involvement has helped me foster a sense of community and has provided opportunities for networking and professional development.

I am fluent in Spanish and English and proficient in programming languages such as C++, Python, Java, etc. Additionally, I have experience using data science libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, and Altair.

As I look forward to graduating, I'm excited to bring my skills and experiences to new challenges in the fields of data science, cybersecurity, and software engineering.
