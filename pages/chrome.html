Chrome Extension for Streamlined Viewing

This project involved the development of a Chrome extension designed to enhance the viewing experience on popular streaming platforms such as Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, and YouTube. The extension automates the process of skipping intros, recaps, and ads, allowing users to dive straight into their desired content.

Before launching the extension, we tested it thoroughly, allowing friends to try it out, which helped enhance its functionality for seamless integration with streaming platforms. This project highlights my technical skills in web development and my commitment to creating enjoyable user experiences.
