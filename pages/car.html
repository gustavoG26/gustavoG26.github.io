Car Dealership Simulator

In a group, I developed a simulation program that manages a virtual vehicle dealership. This project automates inventory management, vehicle repairs, sales transactions, staff operations, and even special events like races, with dynamic updates on vehicle conditions and prices. The system tracks staff responsibilities and maintains vehicles by handling necessary cleaning and repairs. Vehicle stats like condition and cleanliness influence customer interest and sales prices, making for a dynamic and engaging simulation. This project showcases my skills in Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) and the application of design patterns like Factory, Strategy, and Decorator.

This experience not only strengthened my understanding of software architecture but also improved my ability to design systems that are easily flexible for future enhancements. If you are interested in seeing the code or would like to try it out yourself, please feel free to reach out to me!
