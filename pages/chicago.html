Chicago Urban Data Visualization Redesign

Initially this project aimed to uncover the potential relationship between crime rates and the distribution of libraries in Chicago. However, after receiving valuable feedback, it became evident that the initial version of the project fell short of expectations by not providing a clear narrative. As a result, I decided to redesign the project to better communicate the data's story, which involved modifying various elements ranging from color schemes to incorporating completely new datasets.

Through careful analysis, thoughtful design choices, and iterative refinement, I transformed the project into a more engaging and informative experience. To learn more about the journey of this transformation, feel free to read my Medium post, where I provide a detailed account of the redesign process and how the project evolved.
