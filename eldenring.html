Elden Ring Boss Data Analysis

Project Overview: Elden Ring is an action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware, known for its acclaimed titles such as Dark Souls and Bloodborne. These games are typically known for their challenging boss encounters, and Elden Ring is no exception. This project is focused on analyzing the data behind these boss encounters. My aim is to explore the factors and attributes that contribute to the difficulty of these bosses, such as health, rune drop count (which is essentially in-game experience to level up your player), and the number of phases a boss has. By analyzing this data, I hope to uncover the potential relationships between these attributes and provide insights into what makes these bosses so challenging.

Data Collection Journey/Progress: Currently, I am in the process of collecting data related to the bosses in Elden Ring. Despite tedious research, a single comprehensive dataset has yet to be found. As a result, I have been collecting data from various sources such as Reddit and Elden Ring Wikis. I have made great progress in collecting data for the bosses, and I am excited to begin the analysis phase of this project. Once I have collected enough data, I plan to use Python and its libraries such as Pandas and Altair to analyze data and create visualizations that will help me draw conclusions about the data.
